    COMMUNITY

    To create a website that caters to its COMMUNITY effectively, designers must engage in COLLABORATION and COMMUNICATION. This involves seeking input and feedback from the users, as well as maintaining an ongoing dialogue with the community to ensure continuous improvement.

  • 2

    KNOWLEDGE

    Web design encompasses various aspects, including layout, color scheme, graphics, and usability. A knowledgeable web designer knows how to combine these elements harmoniously to create an engaging and visually appealing website.

  • 3

    RESPECT

    Respect is an essential aspect of web design. It plays a significant role in creating a positive user experience for individuals who visit websites. When designing a website, it is crucial to consider the diverse needs and preferences of users. By prioritizing respect in web design, we can ensure a more inclusive and accessible online environment.
